Brand Mentions ROI Estimator

The Brand Mentions ROI Estimator helps you calculate the return on investment (ROI) from brand mentions across various platforms. By analyzing the value generated from brand mentions, you can assess the effectiveness of your brand awareness campaigns.

Formula:

ROI (%) = ((Revenue from Mentions - Cost of Mentions) / Cost of Mentions) × 100

Example:

If your revenue from brand mentions is ₹500,000 and the cost of generating these mentions is ₹100,000, the ROI is:
ROI = ((500,000 - 100,000) / 100,000) × 100 = 400%
