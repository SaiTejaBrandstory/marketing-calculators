Cost Per Thousand Impressions (CPM) in Affiliate Ads

This tool calculates the Cost Per Thousand Impressions (CPM) in affiliate advertising. CPM is a metric used to measure the cost of 1,000 impressions on an ad. It helps advertisers understand how much they are paying for visibility.

Formula:

CPM = (Total Cost / Total Impressions) × 1000

Example:

If an affiliate ad campaign cost **₹5000** and generated **200,000 impressions**, the CPM is calculated as:
CPM = (5000 / 200,000) × 1000 = ₹25
