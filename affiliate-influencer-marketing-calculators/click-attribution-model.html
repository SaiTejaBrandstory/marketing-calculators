Click Attribution Model for Affiliate Marketing

The Click Attribution Model helps track the effectiveness of affiliate marketing campaigns by assigning credit for conversions based on the different touchpoints in the customer journey. This allows marketers to measure how different affiliate channels and campaigns contribute to sales.

Formula:

Attribution Percentage = (Conversion Value from Affiliate Channel / Total Conversion Value) × 100

Example:

If an affiliate channel contributed ₹500 to a total conversion value of ₹2,000, the attribution percentage for that channel would be:
Attribution Percentage = (₹500 / ₹2,000) × 100 = 25%

Total Conversion Value
Conversion Value from Affiliate Channel

FAQ

Click Attribution is the process of assigning credit to the affiliate marketing channel that led to a conversion. This helps in evaluating the effectiveness of different marketing channels.

Attribution Percentage is calculated using the formula:
  • Attribution Percentage = (Conversion Value from Affiliate Channel / Total Conversion Value) × 100
  • It gives the percentage of total conversion value that can be attributed to a specific affiliate channel.
