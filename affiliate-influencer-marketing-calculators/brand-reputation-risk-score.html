Brand Reputation Risk Score for Influencer Partnerships

This calculator helps assess the potential risk to your brand's reputation when partnering with influencers. The score is based on various factors such as audience engagement, influencer credibility, and content quality.

Formula:

Risk Score = (Audience Engagement + Credibility Score + Content Quality) / 3

Example:

If an influencer has an audience engagement score of 70, a credibility score of 80, and a content quality score of 75, the risk score is calculated as:
Risk Score = (70 + 80 + 75) / 3 = 75
