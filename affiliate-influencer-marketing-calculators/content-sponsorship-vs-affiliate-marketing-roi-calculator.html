Content Sponsorship vs. Affiliate Marketing ROI Calculator

This tool helps compare the return on investment (ROI) of content sponsorship versus affiliate marketing. It allows marketers to evaluate which method provides a higher return based on the cost and revenue generated.

Formula:

ROI (%) = (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100

Example for Content Sponsorship:

If the Content Sponsorship cost is ₹10,000 and the revenue generated is ₹15,000, then:
ROI = ((₹15,000 - ₹10,000) / ₹10,000) × 100 = 50.00%

Example for Affiliate Marketing:

If the Affiliate Marketing cost is ₹5,000 and the revenue generated is ₹12,000, then:
ROI = ((₹12,000 - ₹5,000) / ₹5,000) × 100 = 140.00%
