Instagram Story Swipe-Up Rate Calculator

The Instagram Story Swipe-Up Rate Calculator helps influencers and marketers measure the swipe-up engagement rate of their stories. This metric is key to understanding how effectively the call-to-action in a story drives users to the desired link.

Formula:

Swipe-Up Rate (%) = (Total Swipe-Ups / Total Impressions) × 100

Example:

For an Instagram story with 10,000 swipe-ups and 200,000 impressions, the swipe-up rate is calculated as:
Swipe-Up Rate = (10,000 / 200,000) × 100 = 5%

FAQ

The Swipe-Up Rate measures the percentage of users who swipe up on an Instagram story to view the linked content, such as a website, product page, or form.

To improve your Swipe-Up Rate, consider enhancing your story content, making your call-to-action more compelling, and using relevant hashtags or product tags.
