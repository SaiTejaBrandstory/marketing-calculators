FAQ

CTR is calculated using the formula:
  • (Total Clicks / Total Impressions) × 100
  • It represents the percentage of people who clicked the affiliate link compared to the total number of impressions.

Proper link placement on your website is crucial as it directly influences your affiliate link's visibility and the likelihood of a click. Well-placed links increase user engagement and can improve your affiliate earnings.
