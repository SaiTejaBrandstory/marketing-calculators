Brand Loyalty Score via Influencer Marketing

The Brand Loyalty Score via Influencer Marketing helps measure the strength of customer loyalty influenced by marketing campaigns, particularly through influencer partnerships.

Formula:

Brand Loyalty Score = (Repeat Customers / Total Customers) × 100

Example:

If a brand has 500 repeat customers and 1,000 total customers after a successful influencer marketing campaign, the loyalty score is calculated as:
Brand Loyalty Score = (500 / 1,000) × 100 = 50%

Input for Brand Loyalty Score Calculation

Repeat Customers
Total Customers
Brand Loyalty Score (%)

50%
