Performance-Based vs. Fixed Payout Affiliate Model Comparison

This comparison helps you understand the differences between Performance-Based and Fixed Payout affiliate models. Each model has its own advantages depending on the marketing strategy and goals.

Performance-Based Affiliate Model:

In the Performance-Based model, affiliates earn a commission based on the actual sales or actions they generate, such as clicks, purchases, or sign-ups. This model aligns the interests of the merchant and the affiliate, as affiliates are rewarded based on their performance.

Fixed Payout Affiliate Model:

In the Fixed Payout model, affiliates earn a predetermined commission for each sale or action, regardless of the actual performance. This model provides affiliates with consistent payouts, which can be beneficial for those looking for predictable income.
