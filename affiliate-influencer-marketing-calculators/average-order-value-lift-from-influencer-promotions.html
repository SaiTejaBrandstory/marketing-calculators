Average Order Value (AOV) Lift from Influencer Promotions

This tool helps measure the Average Order Value (AOV) lift resulting from influencer promotions. AOV lift indicates the increase in average order value attributed to influencer marketing campaigns.

Formula:

AOV Lift (%) = ((AOV after Influencer Promotion - AOV before Influencer Promotion) / AOV before Influencer Promotion) × 100

Example:

If the AOV before Influencer Promotion is ₹1,500 and the AOV after Influencer Promotion is ₹2,000, then:
AOV Lift = ((₹2,000 - ₹1,500) / ₹1,500) × 100 = 33.33%
