Influencer Audience Authenticity Score

The Influencer Audience Authenticity Score helps brands assess the credibility of an influencer's audience. It measures the percentage of real, engaged followers compared to total followers, which is crucial for determining how effective influencer campaigns are.

Formula:

Authenticity Score (%) = (Engaged Followers / Total Followers) × 100

Example:

If an influencer has 100,000 total followers and 60,000 engaged followers, the authenticity score is calculated as:
Authenticity Score = (60,000 / 100,000) × 100 = 60%
