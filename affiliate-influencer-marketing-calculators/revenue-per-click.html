Revenue Per Click (RPC) in Affiliate Campaigns

This calculator helps you measure the revenue per click generated by your affiliate campaigns, which is a crucial metric for evaluating the profitability of your campaigns.

Formula:

Revenue Per Click (RPC) = Total Revenue / Total Clicks

Example:

If your affiliate campaign generated ₹50,000 in revenue and received 2,000 clicks, the RPC is calculated as:
RPC = ₹50,000 / 2,000 = ₹25
