Lead Magnet Conversion Rate for Affiliate Marketing

This tool helps you measure the conversion rate of your lead magnet in affiliate marketing campaigns. It shows how many visitors convert into leads after engaging with your lead magnet.

Formula:

Lead Magnet Conversion Rate (%) = (Total Leads / Total Visitors) × 100

Example:

If you received 500 leads from 10,000 visitors, the conversion rate is calculated as:
Lead Magnet Conversion Rate = (500 / 10,000) × 100 = 5.00%
