E-Commerce Upsell Rate from Affiliate Traffic

The E-Commerce Upsell Rate from Affiliate Traffic calculator helps you measure how effective your affiliate traffic is in driving upsells.

Formula:

Upsell Rate (%) = (Number of Upsell Transactions / Total Affiliate Transactions) × 100

Example:

If you had 100 total affiliate transactions and 20 upsell transactions, the upsell rate would be:
Upsell Rate = (20 / 100) × 100 = 20%

FAQ

Upsell rate is the percentage of affiliate transactions that result in an upsell, indicating how well your affiliate traffic is generating higher-value purchases.
