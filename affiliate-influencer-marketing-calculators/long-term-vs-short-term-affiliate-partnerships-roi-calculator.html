Long-Term vs. Short-Term Affiliate Partnerships ROI Calculator

This tool compares the ROI of long-term vs short-term affiliate partnerships. It calculates the return on investment based on revenue, costs, and time period.

Formula:

ROI (%) = ((Revenue - Costs) / Costs) × 100

Example:

For a long-term affiliate partnership with ₹1,00,000 revenue and ₹30,000 costs over a year, the ROI is calculated as:
ROI = ((1,00,000 - 30,000) / 30,000) × 100 = 233.33%

FAQ

ROI stands for Return on Investment. It is a measure used to evaluate the efficiency or profitability of an investment, calculated by dividing the profit by the costs and multiplying by 100 to get the percentage.

Long-term partnerships may have higher ROI due to compounded growth over time, while short-term partnerships may show quicker returns but might not generate as much value in the long run.
