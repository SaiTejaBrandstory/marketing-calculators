Micro-Influencer vs. Celebrity Influencer ROI Calculator

This calculator helps compare the Return on Investment (ROI) from Micro-Influencers and Celebrity Influencers. Micro-Influencers often have a smaller but more engaged audience, while Celebrity Influencers have a larger reach but may come with higher costs.

Formula:

ROI = (Revenue from Campaign - Cost of Campaign) / Cost of Campaign × 100

Example for Micro-Influencer:

If you spent ₹100,000 on a Micro-Influencer campaign and earned ₹250,000 in revenue, the ROI would be:
ROI = (₹250,000 - ₹100,000) / ₹100,000 × 100 = 150.00%

Example for Celebrity Influencer:

If you spent ₹500,000 on a Celebrity Influencer campaign and earned ₹800,000 in revenue, the ROI would be:
ROI = (₹800,000 - ₹500,000) / ₹500,000 × 100 = 60.00%

FAQ

ROI (Return on Investment) in influencer marketing measures the profitability of a campaign by comparing the revenue generated to the cost of the campaign.

ROI is calculated using the formula:
  • ROI = (Revenue from Campaign - Cost of Campaign) / Cost of Campaign × 100
  • It measures the profit generated for every unit of currency spent on the campaign.
