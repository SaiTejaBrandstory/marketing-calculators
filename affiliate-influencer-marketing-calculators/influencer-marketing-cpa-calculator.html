Influencer Marketing Cost per Acquisition (CPA) Calculator

The Influencer Marketing CPA Calculator helps you determine the cost per acquisition (CPA) of your influencer marketing campaigns. CPA is an important metric to understand how much you are paying to acquire each customer through your influencer partnerships.

Formula:

CPA = Total Campaign Cost / Number of Acquisitions

Example:

If you spent ₹50,000 on an influencer campaign and acquired 200 customers, the CPA would be:
CPA = ₹50,000 / 200 = ₹250 per customer.
