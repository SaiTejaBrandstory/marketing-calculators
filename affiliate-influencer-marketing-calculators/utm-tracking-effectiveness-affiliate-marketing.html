UTM Tracking Effectiveness in Affiliate Marketing

The UTM Tracking Effectiveness tool helps affiliate marketers track the performance of their campaigns using UTM parameters. This enables better insights into which affiliate links and sources are driving the most traffic and conversions.

Formula:

UTM Effectiveness (%) = (Total Conversions from UTM Links / Total Conversions) × 100

Example:

If your affiliate marketing campaign received 500 conversions from 2,000 visitors, and 200 conversions were from UTM-tracked links, the UTM effectiveness is:
UTM Effectiveness = (200 / 500) × 100 = 40%
