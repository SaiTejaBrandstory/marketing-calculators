Try different inputs to see how small changes in commission rates or bonuses affect your total earnings. Stay informed and optimize your affiliate marketing approach for greater success!

Formula:

Commission Earnings = (Total Sales × Commission Rate) + Bonus

Example:

Suppose you generate ₹100,000 in total sales with a 12% commission rate and an additional performance bonus of ₹8,000. The earnings calculation will be:
Commission Earnings = (₹100,000 × 12%) + ₹8,000 = ₹12,000 + ₹8,000 = ₹20,000
