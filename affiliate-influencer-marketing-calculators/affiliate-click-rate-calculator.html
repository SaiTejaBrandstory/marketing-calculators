Social Media Affiliate Click Rate Calculator

The Social Media Affiliate Click Rate Calculator helps marketers measure the percentage of clicks generated from affiliate links. This is crucial for understanding the effectiveness of affiliate campaigns and social media promotions.

Click rate is a key performance metric in affiliate marketing, indicating how many users are clicking on your affiliate links relative to the total impressions. A higher click rate suggests better engagement and interest from the audience.

Formula:

Click Rate (%) = (Total Clicks / Total Impressions) × 100

Example:

If your affiliate link received **2,000 clicks** from **50,000 impressions**, the click rate is calculated as:
Click Rate = (2,000 / 50,000) × 100 = **4%**
