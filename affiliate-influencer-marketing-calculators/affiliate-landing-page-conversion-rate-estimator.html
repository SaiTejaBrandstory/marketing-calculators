Affiliate Landing Page Conversion Rate Estimator

The Affiliate Landing Page Conversion Rate Estimator helps you evaluate how effective your landing page is at converting visitors into customers. It measures the percentage of visitors who take a desired action after visiting the page.

Formula:

Conversion Rate (%) = (Total Conversions / Total Visitors) × 100

Example:

If your landing page had 5,000 visitors and achieved 200 conversions, the conversion rate is calculated as:
Conversion Rate = (200 / 5,000) × 100 = 4%
