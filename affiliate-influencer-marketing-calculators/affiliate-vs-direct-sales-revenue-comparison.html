Affiliate vs. Direct Sales Revenue Comparison

The Affiliate vs. Direct Sales Revenue Comparison helps businesses analyze and compare revenue generated from affiliate marketing and direct sales. This comparison is important to evaluate the profitability and efficiency of different sales channels.

Formula:

Revenue Comparison = (Affiliate Revenue / Direct Sales Revenue) × 100

Example:

If the affiliate revenue is ₹50,000 and the direct sales revenue is ₹200,000, the revenue comparison is calculated as:
Revenue Comparison = (50,000 / 200,000) × 100 = 25%
