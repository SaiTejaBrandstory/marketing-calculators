Click Fraud Risk in Affiliate Marketing

The Click Fraud Risk in Affiliate Marketing helps identify the likelihood of fraudulent clicks on affiliate links. Understanding the risk can help reduce wasted spend and improve campaign performance.

Formula:

Click Fraud Risk (%) = (Suspicious Clicks / Total Clicks) × 100

Example:

If an affiliate campaign received 1,500 suspicious clicks out of 50,000 total clicks, the click fraud risk is calculated as:
Click Fraud Risk = (1,500 / 50,000) × 100 = 3%

Input for Click Fraud Risk Calculation

Suspicious Clicks
Total Clicks
