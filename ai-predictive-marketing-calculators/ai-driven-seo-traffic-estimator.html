AI-Driven SEO Traffic Estimator

This estimator calculates your expected website traffic based on your SEO efforts such as keyword optimization, backlinks, and content relevance.

Formula:

The SEO traffic is estimated based on the following formula:

  • Estimated Traffic = (Keyword Optimization × 0.5) + (Backlinks × 0.3) + (Content Relevance × 0.2)
Example Calculation:

If the values are:

  • Keyword Optimization: 80
  • Backlinks: 60
  • Content Relevance: 70

The estimated traffic would be:

(80 × 0.5) + (60 × 0.3) + (70 × 0.2) = 40 + 18 + 14 = 72
