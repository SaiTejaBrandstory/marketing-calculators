AI-Based Demand Forecasting Tool

The AI-Based Demand Forecasting Tool utilizes machine learning algorithms to predict future demand based on historical data, seasonal trends, and market conditions. By inputting relevant data such as historical sales and seasonality factors, the tool predicts demand for future periods to help businesses plan better.

Formula:

The formula for demand forecasting can be expressed as:

Demand Forecast = (Historical Sales × Trend Factor) + (Seasonal Adjustment)

Example Calculation:

If the values are:

  • Historical Sales: 1000 units
  • Trend Factor: 1.2
  • Seasonal Adjustment: 50 units

The demand forecast would be:

((1000 × 1.2) + 50) = 1250 units

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Demand forecasting is the process of predicting future customer demand for a product or service using historical data, market trends, and other factors.

The forecast is calculated by multiplying historical sales with a trend factor and adding any seasonal adjustments to obtain the final demand forecast.

Accurate demand forecasting helps businesses optimize inventory, reduce costs, and improve customer satisfaction by ensuring they can meet future demand without overstocking.
