AI-Based Lead Nurturing ROI Calculator

The AI-Based Lead Nurturing ROI Calculator helps businesses determine the return on investment (ROI) of their AI-driven lead nurturing efforts. By calculating the costs and the outcomes from nurturing leads, it shows how effective AI-powered strategies are at improving conversion rates and sales.

Formula:

ROI (%) = ((Revenue from Nurtured Leads - Total Cost of Nurturing) / Total Cost of Nurturing) * 100

Example Calculation:

If the revenue from nurtured leads is ₹500,000 and the total cost of nurturing is ₹100,000, then:

ROI (%) = ((500,000 - 100,000) / 100,000) * 100 = 400%
