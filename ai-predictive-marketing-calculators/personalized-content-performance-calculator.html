Personalized Content Performance Calculator

The Personalized Content Performance Calculator helps evaluate the effectiveness of personalized content in driving engagement and conversions. By inputting engagement, conversion, and audience targeting data, you can determine the impact of personalized content versus generic content.

Formula:

The formula to calculate content performance is:

Content Performance = (Engagement Rate * 0.4) + (Conversion Rate * 0.4) + (Audience Targeting Score * 0.2)

Example Calculation:

If the values are:

  • Engagement Rate: 75%
  • Conversion Rate: 60%
  • Audience Targeting Score: 80%

The content performance would be:

((75 * 0.4) + (60 * 0.4) + (80 * 0.2)) = 70%
