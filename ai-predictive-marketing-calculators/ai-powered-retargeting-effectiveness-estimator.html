Formula:

The formula for retargeting effectiveness is:

Retargeting Effectiveness = (Number of Conversions from Retargeting / Total Retargeted Users) × 100

Example Calculation:

If the values are:

  • Number of Conversions from Retargeting: 120
  • Total Retargeted Users: 800

The effectiveness would be:

((120 / 800) × 100) = 15%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Retargeting effectiveness refers to the percentage of users who were retargeted with ads and then converted into customers, showing the success of the retargeting strategy.

The effectiveness is calculated by dividing the number of conversions from retargeted users by the total number of retargeted users and multiplying the result by 100 to get the percentage.

This metric helps businesses understand the success of their retargeting campaigns. A higher effectiveness rate indicates that the retargeting strategy is resonating well with the target audience.
