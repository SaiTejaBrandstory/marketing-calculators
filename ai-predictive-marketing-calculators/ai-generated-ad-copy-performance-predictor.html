AI-Generated Ad Copy Performance Predictor

This tool estimates the effectiveness of an AI-generated ad copy based on key performance indicators (KPIs). It helps predict how the ad copy would perform based on metrics such as click-through rate (CTR), conversion rate, and engagement level.

Formula:

The ad copy performance is estimated by combining factors like click-through rate, conversion rate, and engagement level.

Ad Performance Score = (CTR × 0.4) + (Conversion Rate × 0.4) + (Engagement Level × 0.2)

Example Calculation:

If the values are:

  • CTR: 3.5%
  • Conversion Rate: 2.8%
  • Engagement Level: 70%

The ad performance score would be:

((3.5 × 0.4) + (2.8 × 0.4) + (70 × 0.2)) = 16.52

The Ad Performance Score predicts how well an AI-generated ad copy will perform, based on key performance metrics like CTR, conversion rate, and engagement level.
