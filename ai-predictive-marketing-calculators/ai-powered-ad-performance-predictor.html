AI-Powered Ad Performance Predictor

The AI-Powered Ad Performance Predictor helps estimate the return on investment (ROI) for your advertising campaigns based on factors like ad spend, conversion rate, and average order value. Below is an example calculation:

Example Calculation:

Ad Spend (₹): ₹5000

Conversion Rate (%): 2%

Average Order Value (₹): ₹1500

Formula:

The formula for calculating ROI is:

  • ROI = (Revenue Generated - Ad Spend) / Ad Spend * 100
Step-by-step Calculation:

Revenue Generated: ₹5000 × (2 / 100) × ₹1500 = ₹150,000

ROI: (₹150,000 - ₹5000) / ₹5000 * 100 = 2900%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

ROI (Return on Investment) in advertising measures the profitability of your advertising spend. A positive ROI indicates your ad spend is generating profit, while a negative ROI means the ad is not generating enough revenue.

Estimated revenue is calculated by multiplying the conversion rate (%) by the ad spend and the average order value.

ROI helps you understand the profitability of your advertising campaigns. A higher ROI means your ads are effective, while a lower ROI may require optimization of your strategy.
