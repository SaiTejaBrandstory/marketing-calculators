Loyalty Program ROI Calculator

Formula:

The formula for calculating the Loyalty Program ROI is:

ROI = (Revenue from Loyal Customers - Cost of Program) / Cost of Program

Example Calculation:

If:

  • Revenue from Loyal Customers = ₹500,000
  • Cost of Program = ₹100,000

ROI = (500,000 - 100,000) / 100,000 = 400,000 / 100,000 = 4.00

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

The Loyalty Program ROI calculates the return on investment from a loyalty program by comparing the revenue generated from loyal customers to the cost of running the program.

This ROI metric helps businesses evaluate the effectiveness of their loyalty programs. A high ROI means the program is profitable and worth continuing, while a low ROI indicates a need for adjustments.

You can improve your ROI by increasing the revenue from loyal customers through targeted promotions or improving customer retention rates, while minimizing the costs of the program.
