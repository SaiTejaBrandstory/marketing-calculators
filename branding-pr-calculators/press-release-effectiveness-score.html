Press Release Effectiveness Score Calculator

The Press Release Effectiveness Score (PRES) evaluates the effectiveness of your press release based on audience reach, engagement, and media coverage.

Formula:

Press Release Effectiveness Score = (Audience Reach + Engagement Score + Media Coverage) / 3

Example Calculation:

Audience Reach: 80

Engagement Score: 70

Media Coverage: 90

Press Release Effectiveness Score = (80 + 70 + 90) / 3 = 80.00
