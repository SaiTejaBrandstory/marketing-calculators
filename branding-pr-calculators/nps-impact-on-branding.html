Net Promoter Score (NPS) Impact on Branding

The Net Promoter Score (NPS) measures customer satisfaction and loyalty by asking customers how likely they are to recommend a brand to others. It can significantly impact brand perception.

Formula:

NPS = % of Promoters - % of Detractors

Example Calculation:

Promoters (%): 70

Detractors (%): 20

NPS = 70 - 20 = 50
