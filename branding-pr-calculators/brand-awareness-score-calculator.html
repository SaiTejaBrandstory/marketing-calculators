Brand Awareness Score Calculator

The Brand Awareness Score Calculator helps you assess how well your brand is recognized in the market. It considers social media mentions, website traffic, and survey responses.

Formula:

Brand Awareness Score = (Social Media Mentions + Website Traffic + Survey Responses) / 3

Example Calculation:

Social Media Mentions: 150

Website Traffic: 5000

Survey Responses: 100

Brand Awareness Score = (150 + 5000 + 100) / 3 = 1750.00
