Celebrity Endorsement ROI Calculator

Calculate the Return on Investment (ROI) for your celebrity endorsement campaign based on the total cost, brand revenue increase, and awareness boost.

Formula:

ROI = (Brand Revenue Increase - Celebrity Endorsement Cost) / Celebrity Endorsement Cost * 100

Example Calculation:

Celebrity Endorsement Cost: ₹2,00,000

Brand Revenue Increase: ₹10,00,000

ROI = (10,00,000 - 2,00,000) / 2,00,000 * 100 = 400%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Celebrity Endorsement ROI is a measure of the return a brand gets from its investment in a celebrity endorsement campaign, considering the increase in brand revenue relative to the cost of the endorsement.

The formula for ROI is: ROI = (Brand Revenue Increase - Celebrity Endorsement Cost) / Celebrity Endorsement Cost * 100.

ROI is important as it helps assess the effectiveness of the endorsement campaign. A high ROI indicates that the endorsement was a good investment for the brand.
