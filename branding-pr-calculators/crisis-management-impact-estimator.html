Crisis Management Impact Estimator

The Crisis Management Impact Estimator calculates the potential financial impact of a crisis and the resources needed to mitigate the damage.

Formula:

Crisis Management Impact = (Revenue Loss + Legal Costs + PR Efforts + Operational Costs)

Example Calculation:

Revenue Loss: ₹500,000

Legal Costs: ₹100,000

PR Efforts: ₹200,000

Operational Costs: ₹150,000

Crisis Management Impact = ₹500,000 + ₹100,000 + ₹200,000 + ₹150,000 = ₹950,000

Input for Crisis Management Impact

Revenue Loss (₹)
Legal Costs (₹)
PR Efforts (₹)
Operational Costs (₹)
Total Crisis Management Impact (₹)

₹950,000
