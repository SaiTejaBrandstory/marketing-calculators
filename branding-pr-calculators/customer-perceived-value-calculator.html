Customer Perceived Value of a Brand

This calculator helps to evaluate the perceived value of a brand based on customer assessments of product quality, price, and service.

Formula:

Customer Perceived Value = (Product Quality + Service Quality - Price) / 2

Example Calculation:

Product Quality: 80

Service Quality: 90

Price: 70

Customer Perceived Value = (80 + 90 - 70) / 2 = 50.00

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Customer Perceived Value is the customer's overall assessment of a product's quality, service, and its price relative to the benefits.

The formula is: (Product Quality + Service Quality - Price) / 2.

A high customer perceived value indicates a strong competitive edge, customer satisfaction, and loyalty towards the brand.
