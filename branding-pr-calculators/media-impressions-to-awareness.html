Media impressions refer to the number of times an advertisement or content is displayed or seen by the target audience.

The conversion rate is the percentage of media impressions that successfully lead to increased brand awareness.

This tool helps marketers estimate the effectiveness of their media campaigns and understand how many impressions are needed to achieve a certain level of brand awareness.
