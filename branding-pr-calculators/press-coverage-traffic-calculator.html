Press Coverage Impact on Website Traffic Calculator

This calculator helps measure the impact of press coverage on your website traffic, factoring in the number of press mentions and average traffic increase after each press mention.

Formula:

Impact on Website Traffic = (Press Mentions * Average Traffic Increase per Mention)

Example Calculation:

Press Mentions: 5

Average Traffic Increase per Mention: 200

Impact on Website Traffic = 5 * 200 = 1000 visitors
