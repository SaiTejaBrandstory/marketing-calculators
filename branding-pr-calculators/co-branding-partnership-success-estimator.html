Co-Branding Partnership Success Estimator

This estimator helps evaluate the potential success of a co-branding partnership by assessing factors such as alignment of target audiences, brand reputation, and market reach.

Formula:

Co-Branding Success Score = (Target Audience Alignment + Brand Reputation + Market Reach) / 3

Example Calculation:

Target Audience Alignment: 80

Brand Reputation: 70

Market Reach: 90

Co-Branding Success Score = (80 + 70 + 90) / 3 = 80.00
