Product Packaging Influence on Brand Perception

This tool helps evaluate the influence of product packaging on the brand perception based on factors like packaging design, quality, and sustainability.

Formula:

Brand Perception Score = (Design Score + Quality Score + Sustainability Score) / 3

Example Calculation:

Design Score: 80

Quality Score: 85

Sustainability Score: 70

Brand Perception Score = (80 + 85 + 70) / 3 = 78.33
