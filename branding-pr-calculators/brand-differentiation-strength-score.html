Brand Differentiation Strength Score

The Brand Differentiation Strength Score measures how distinct your brand is compared to competitors in terms of product uniqueness, market positioning, and customer perception.

Formula:

Brand Differentiation Strength Score = (Product Uniqueness + Market Positioning + Customer Perception) / 3

Example Calculation:

Product Uniqueness: 75

Market Positioning: 80

Customer Perception: 70

Brand Differentiation Strength Score = (75 + 80 + 70) / 3 = 75.00
