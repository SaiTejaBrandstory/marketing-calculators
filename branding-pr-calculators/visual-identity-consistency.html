Visual Identity Consistency Score Calculator

The Visual Identity Consistency Score measures how consistently your brand's visual elements are applied across different channels.

Formula:

Visual Identity Consistency Score = (Logo Consistency + Color Scheme Consistency + Typography Consistency) / 3

Example Calculation:

Logo Consistency: 85

Color Scheme Consistency: 90

Typography Consistency: 80

Visual Identity Consistency Score = (85 + 90 + 80) / 3 = 85.00
