Brand Positioning Strength Calculator

The Brand Positioning Strength Calculator helps assess the effectiveness of your brand’s positioning in the market by evaluating key factors like Customer Perception, Market Differentiation, and Emotional Connection.

Formula:

Brand Positioning Strength = (Customer Perception + Market Differentiation + Emotional Connection) / 3
