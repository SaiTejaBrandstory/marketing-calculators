Brand Hashtag Engagement Rate Calculator

The Brand Hashtag Engagement Rate Calculator helps you measure the engagement on social media related to a specific brand hashtag. It calculates the engagement rate by comparing the number of interactions to the number of impressions or reach.

Formula:

Engagement Rate = (Total Interactions / Total Impressions) * 100

Example Calculation:

Total Interactions: 2000

Total Impressions: 50000

Engagement Rate = (2000 / 50000) * 100 = 4%
