Social Proof Strength Index Calculator

The Social Proof Strength Index (SPSI) measures the strength of a brand's social proof based on customer reviews, testimonials, social media mentions, and influencer endorsements.

Formula:

Social Proof Strength Index = (Customer Reviews Score + Social Media Mentions + Influencer Endorsement Score) / 3

Example Calculation:

Customer Reviews Score: 80

Social Media Mentions: 70

Influencer Endorsement Score: 90

Social Proof Strength Index = (80 + 70 + 90) / 3 = 80.00
