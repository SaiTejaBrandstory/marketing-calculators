Chatbot Lead Conversion Rate Calculator

The Chatbot Lead Conversion Rate Calculator helps you measure how effectively your chatbot is converting website visitors into leads. This tool takes into account the number of visitors interacting with the chatbot and the number of successful leads generated.

By adjusting the visitor and lead numbers, you can evaluate the performance of your chatbot and optimize it to increase conversion rates. A higher conversion rate means better performance in capturing leads through automated interactions.

Formula:

Conversion Rate = (Leads / Chatbot Interactions) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Leads: 150
  • Chatbot Interactions: 1000
  • Conversion Rate: (150 / 1000) × 100 = 15.00%
