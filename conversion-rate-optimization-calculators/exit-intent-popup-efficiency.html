Exit Intent Popup Efficiency Calculator

This calculator helps you measure the efficiency of your exit intent popups in terms of conversion rate. By comparing the number of conversions generated by the exit intent popup to the number of users who were shown the popup, you can assess its effectiveness in reducing cart abandonment or encouraging conversions before users leave the page.

Enter the number of users who saw the popup, the number of conversions, and the total number of visitors to determine how well your exit intent popup is performing.

Formula:

Popup Conversion Rate = (Conversions / Users Who Saw Popup) × 100

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

An exit intent popup is a marketing tool that detects when a user is about to leave a website and displays a targeted message or offer to encourage the user to stay or complete a desired action, such as making a purchase.

Exit intent popups help reduce cart abandonment and encourage users to complete actions they might have otherwise abandoned. By offering discounts, reminding users of items in their cart, or presenting a special offer, you can motivate users to stay and convert.

A good exit intent popup conversion rate varies by industry, but a conversion rate of 2% to 5% is considered typical. However, conversion rates can be significantly higher with well-designed popups and compelling offers.
