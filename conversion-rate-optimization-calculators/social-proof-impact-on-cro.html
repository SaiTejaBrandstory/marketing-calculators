Social Proof Impact on CRO Calculator

This calculator helps you evaluate how social proof impacts your conversion rate optimization (CRO). Social proof includes elements like customer testimonials, product reviews, or social media mentions that can increase trust and influence a visitor's decision to convert.

Enter the number of visitors before social proof and after implementing social proof, along with the number of conversions for both groups. The tool will calculate the percentage increase in conversion rate and demonstrate the impact of social proof on conversions.

Formula:

Conversion Rate Increase = ((After Conversion Rate - Before Conversion Rate) / Before Conversion Rate) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Before Visitors: 1000
  • Before Conversions: 50
  • After Visitors: 1200
  • After Conversions: 90
  • Conversion Rate Before: (50 / 1000) × 100 = 5.00%
  • Conversion Rate After: (90 / 1200) × 100 = 7.50%
  • Conversion Rate Increase: ((7.50 - 5.00) / 5.00) × 100 = 50.00%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Social proof is a psychological phenomenon where people are more likely to take action if they see others doing the same. In marketing, this can include customer reviews, testimonials, social media mentions, and more to build trust and encourage conversions.

Social proof can significantly impact conversion rates by increasing trust and credibility. When potential customers see that others are satisfied with a product or service, they are more likely to convert themselves.

Types of social proof that can improve conversion rates include customer testimonials, product reviews, ratings, case studies, influencer endorsements, and social media mentions. The more relatable and credible the social proof, the greater its impact on conversions.
