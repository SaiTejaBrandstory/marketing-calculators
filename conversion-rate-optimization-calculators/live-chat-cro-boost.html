Live Chat Implementation CRO Boost Calculator

This calculator helps you assess the impact of live chat implementation on your Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO). By incorporating live chat, you can engage visitors in real-time, answer their questions, and guide them toward making a purchase. This tool estimates how much live chat can boost your conversion rate based on your input data.

Implementing live chat can help reduce bounce rates and increase engagement, directly impacting your conversion rate. With this tool, you can measure the effectiveness of live chat and predict the potential improvements in conversion.

Formula:

Conversion Rate Increase = (Live Chat Conversion Rate - No Live Chat Conversion Rate) / No Live Chat Conversion Rate × 100

Example Calculation:
  • No Live Chat Conversion Rate: 3%
  • Live Chat Conversion Rate: 5%
  • Conversion Rate Increase: (5 - 3) / 3 × 100 = 66.67%
