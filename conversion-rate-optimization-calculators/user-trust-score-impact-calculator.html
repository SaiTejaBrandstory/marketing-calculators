User Trust Score Impact on Conversion Rate Calculator

The User Trust Score Impact on Conversion Rate Calculator helps you understand how the trust level of your users impacts their likelihood to convert. A higher trust score generally leads to a higher conversion rate.

User trust can be built through factors like website security, transparency, reviews, and user experiences. This calculator will help you see how different trust scores affect your conversion rate.

Formula:

Conversion Rate = Base Conversion Rate × (1 + Trust Score Impact Factor)

Example Calculation:
  • Base Conversion Rate: 5%
  • Trust Score Impact Factor: 0.10
  • Calculated Conversion Rate: 5% × (1 + 0.10) = 5.50%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Trust directly influences the likelihood that a user will complete a conversion action. The more trust a user has in a brand, the more likely they are to purchase, subscribe, or take other desired actions on the website.

You can increase trust by displaying customer reviews, using secure payment options, providing transparent policies, offering guarantees, and ensuring fast, helpful customer support.
