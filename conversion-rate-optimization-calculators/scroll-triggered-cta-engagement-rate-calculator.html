Scroll Triggered CTA Engagement Rate Calculator

The Scroll Triggered CTA Engagement Rate Calculator helps you determine how effective your scroll-triggered call-to-action (CTA) is in engaging visitors. This metric measures the percentage of users who interacted with your CTA after it was triggered by their scroll position on the page.

A higher engagement rate suggests that your CTA is well-placed and resonates with users as they engage with the content. By tracking and optimizing this metric, you can enhance your website’s conversion potential.

Formula:

Engagement Rate = (CTA Interactions / Total Scrolls) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • CTA Interactions: 120
  • Total Scrolls: 1500
  • Engagement Rate: (120 / 1500) × 100 = 8.00%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Scroll Triggered CTA Engagement Rate measures the effectiveness of your CTA that is triggered based on the user’s scroll depth. It shows how many users engaged with the CTA after it was displayed during their page scroll.

To improve engagement, you should ensure your CTA is relevant, visually appealing, and positioned at the right scroll depth. Additionally, testing different variations and content messaging can help optimize performance.
