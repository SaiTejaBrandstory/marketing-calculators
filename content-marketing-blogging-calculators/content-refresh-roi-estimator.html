Content Refresh ROI Estimator

This tool estimates the Return on Investment (ROI) of refreshing existing content to improve performance. Simply enter the data for the updated content's revenue and cost.

Formula:

ROI (%) = ((Revenue from Updated Content - Cost of Content Refresh) / Cost of Content Refresh) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Revenue from Updated Content: ₹8,000
  • Cost of Content Refresh: ₹1,500
  • ROI: ((8,000 - 1,500) / 1,500) × 100 = 433.33%

FAQ

Content Refresh ROI refers to the return on investment when updating or refreshing old content to improve its performance. The goal is to drive more traffic, engagement, or conversions.

To calculate Content Refresh ROI:
  • Determine the revenue generated by the updated content after refreshing it.
  • Calculate the total cost of refreshing the content (e.g., rewriting, design updates, promotion).
  • Use the formula: ROI (%) = ((Revenue from Updated Content - Cost of Content Refresh) / Cost of Content Refresh) × 100.
