Skyscraper Content ROI Calculator

This tool helps you calculate the ROI of your Skyscraper Content by factoring in traffic generation, engagement, and conversion.

Formula:

ROI (%) = ((Revenue from Content - Content Production Cost) / Content Production Cost) * 100

Example Calculation:
  • Revenue from Content: ₹20,000
  • Content Production Cost: ₹8,000
  • ROI: ((₹20,000 - ₹8,000) / ₹8,000) * 100 = 150%

FAQ

Skyscraper Content refers to high-quality, in-depth content created with the goal of outranking existing content on a specific topic. It is often more comprehensive and well-researched than the competition.

To improve ROI:
  • Ensure the content is comprehensive and well-researched to outperform competitors.
  • Optimize for SEO to drive more organic traffic.
  • Promote the content through various channels (email, social media, etc.) to increase engagement.
  • Ensure the content is actionable and valuable to drive conversions.
