Comment Engagement Rate on Blog Posts

This calculator helps you evaluate the comment engagement rate on your blog posts based on the number of comments and the total views.

Formula:

Engagement Rate (%) = (Number of Comments / Number of Views) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Number of Comments: 50
  • Number of Views: 1000
  • Engagement Rate: (50 / 1000) × 100 = 5%

FAQ

The comment engagement rate gives insights into how engaged your readers are with your content. High engagement often indicates that your content resonates with your audience, leading to better user retention and more organic growth.

To improve comment engagement, encourage discussions by asking questions in your blog posts, replying to comments, and creating content that invites conversation. You can also engage with readers through social media and newsletters to drive more traffic to your blog.
