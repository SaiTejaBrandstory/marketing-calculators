AI-Generated vs. Human Content Engagement

This calculator helps you compare engagement metrics between AI-generated and human-generated content, providing insights into which content type performs better.

Formula:

Engagement Rate (%) = (Engagement / Total Reach) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Engagement (AI): 500
  • Total Reach (AI): 10000
  • Engagement Rate (AI): (500 / 10000) × 100 = 5%
  • Engagement (Human): 700
  • Total Reach (Human): 10000
  • Engagement Rate (Human): (700 / 10000) × 100 = 7%

FAQ

Engagement Rate is a metric that measures the level of interaction (likes, shares, comments, etc.) with a piece of content relative to its reach (the number of people who have seen it).

This tool calculates the engagement rate of both AI-generated and human-generated content, and compares their performance.

To improve engagement:
  • Optimize content for your target audience
  • Use compelling headlines and calls to action
  • Engage with users through comments and feedback
  • Publish consistently and strategically
