Video vs. Text Content ROI Calculator

This calculator helps you estimate the ROI of Video vs. Text content based on views, engagement, and costs.

Formula:

ROI (%) = ((Revenue Generated - Total Cost) / Total Cost) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Revenue Generated: ₹5000
  • Total Cost: ₹2000
  • ROI: ((5000 - 2000) / 2000) × 100 = 150%

FAQ

ROI is calculated by subtracting the total cost from the revenue generated, dividing the result by the total cost, and then multiplying by 100 to get the percentage.

The ROI of video vs. text content depends on several factors, including audience engagement, content quality, and production costs. Generally, videos may have a higher production cost but can generate more engagement, while text content is more cost-effective.
