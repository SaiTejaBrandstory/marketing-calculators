Content Cluster Effectiveness Score Calculator

This tool evaluates the effectiveness of your content cluster strategy based on organic traffic, backlinks, and internal link strength.

Formula:

Effectiveness Score (%) = [(Organic Traffic × 0.5) + (Backlinks × 0.3) + (Internal Links × 0.2)]

Example:

Cluster A: Organic Traffic = 10,000, Backlinks = 200, Internal Links = 50

Cluster B: Organic Traffic = 7,500, Backlinks = 150, Internal Links = 80

Effectiveness Score for Cluster A: (10,000 × 0.5) + (200 × 0.3) + (50 × 0.2) = 5,000 + 60 + 10 = 5,070

Effectiveness Score for Cluster B: (7,500 × 0.5) + (150 × 0.3) + (80 × 0.2) = 3,750 + 45 + 16 = 3,811

FAQ

Content Cluster Effectiveness refers to how well your content groups perform based on the combined metrics of organic traffic, backlinks, and internal links.

The Effectiveness Score is calculated as follows:
  • Organic Traffic × 0.5
  • Backlinks × 0.3
  • Internal Links × 0.2
  • Sum them up to get the final effectiveness score.
