AI-Written Blog Post vs. Human-Written Performance Calculator

This tool compares the performance of AI-generated and human-written blog posts based on views, engagement, and conversion rates.

Formula:

Performance Score (%) = (Views × 0.4) + (Engagement × 0.3) + (Conversions × 0.3)

Example:

AI-Written Blog Post:
Views = 10,000, Engagement = 500, Conversions = 200
Performance Score = (10,000 × 0.4) + (500 × 0.3) + (200 × 0.3) = 4,000 + 150 + 60 = 4,210

Human-Written Blog Post:
Views = 12,000, Engagement = 800, Conversions = 300
Performance Score = (12,000 × 0.4) + (800 × 0.3) + (300 × 0.3) = 4,800 + 240 + 90 = 5,130

FAQ

The Performance Score is a metric that evaluates the performance of a blog post based on views, engagement, and conversions. It is a weighted sum of these three metrics.

The Performance Score is calculated as follows:
  • Views × 0.4
  • Engagement × 0.3
  • Conversions × 0.3
  • Sum them up to get the final performance score.
