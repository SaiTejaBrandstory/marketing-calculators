Localized Marketing Insights Score

This calculator evaluates the effectiveness of localized marketing strategies based on various factors.

Formula:

The score is calculated as:

  • Localized Marketing Insights Score = ( (Cultural Relevance × 0.4) + (Local Engagement × 0.3) + (Market Adaptability × 0.3) )
Example Calculation:

For the following data:

  • Cultural Relevance: 85
  • Local Engagement: 75
  • Market Adaptability: 80

Score:

( (85 × 0.4) + (75 × 0.3) + (80 × 0.3) ) = 80.5

Input for Localized Marketing Insights Score

Cultural Relevance (0-100)
Local Engagement (0-100)
Market Adaptability (0-100)
