Consumer Behavioral Insights Score Calculator

This calculator helps you evaluate the Consumer Behavioral Insights Score based on various factors such as purchase frequency, purchase volume, and customer engagement level.

Formula:

The Behavioral Insights Score is calculated using the following formula:

  • Behavioral Insights Score = (Purchase Frequency * Purchase Volume * Customer Engagement Level) / 1000
Example Calculation:

If the following data is provided:

  • Purchase Frequency: 5 purchases per month
  • Purchase Volume: ₹1000
  • Customer Engagement Level: 80

The Behavioral Insights Score would be:

Behavioral Insights Score = (5 * 1000 * 80) / 1000 = 400

Input for Consumer Behavioral Insights Calculation

Purchase Frequency (per month)
Purchase Volume (₹)
Customer Engagement Level
Behavioral Insights Score

400

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

The Consumer Behavioral Insights Score is a measure of consumer behavior, incorporating frequency of purchases, volume spent, and engagement with your brand.

The score is calculated by multiplying the purchase frequency, purchase volume, and customer engagement level, and then dividing the result by 1000.

The score helps businesses understand consumer purchasing behavior, engagement levels, and loyalty, providing insights into potential strategies for improving sales.
