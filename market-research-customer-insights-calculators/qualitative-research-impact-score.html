Qualitative Research Impact Score

This tool calculates the impact of your qualitative research by evaluating participant engagement, insights gained, and actionable outcomes.

Formula:

The Qualitative Research Impact Score is calculated by considering the following factors:

  • Participant Engagement (0-10)
  • Insights Gained (0-10)
  • Actionable Outcomes (0-10)

The score is calculated as the average of these factors:

  • Impact Score = (Participant Engagement + Insights Gained + Actionable Outcomes) / 3
Example Calculation:

If you have the following scores for each factor:

  • Participant Engagement: 8
  • Insights Gained: 7
  • Actionable Outcomes: 9

The Impact Score would be:

  • Impact Score = (8 + 7 + 9) / 3 = 8.00

Input for Qualitative Research Impact Calculation

Participant Engagement (0-10)
Insights Gained (0-10)
Actionable Outcomes (0-10)
Impact Score

8.00
