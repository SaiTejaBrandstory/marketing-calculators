Loyalty Program Performance Benchmark

This calculator evaluates the performance of your loyalty program based on key metrics.

Formula:

The score is calculated using the following formula:

Performance Score = (Participation Rate × 0.4) + (Redemption Rate × 0.3) + (Customer Retention Rate × 0.3)

Example Calculation:

For example, if the values are:

  • Participation Rate: 40%
  • Redemption Rate: 25%
  • Customer Retention Rate: 80%

The Performance Score would be: (40 × 0.4) + (25 × 0.3) + (80 × 0.3) = 16 + 7.5 + 24 = 47.5

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

The Loyalty Program Performance Benchmark helps businesses evaluate the effectiveness of their loyalty program based on customer participation, redemption rates, and retention rates.

The Performance Score is calculated by multiplying the Participation Rate by 0.4, the Redemption Rate by 0.3, and the Retention Rate by 0.3, then adding the results together.

This benchmark helps businesses assess the impact of their loyalty programs and make data-driven decisions to enhance customer loyalty and retention.
