Market Readiness Score for New Product Launch

This calculator evaluates how ready the market is for a new product launch based on key factors.

Formula:

The Market Readiness Score is calculated as:

  • Market Readiness Score = ( (Market Demand × 0.4) + (Competition Level × 0.3) + (Consumer Awareness × 0.3) )
Example Calculation:

For the following data:

  • Market Demand: 80
  • Competition Level: 60
  • Consumer Awareness: 70

Score:

( (80 × 0.4) + (60 × 0.3) + (70 × 0.3) ) = 71

Input for Market Readiness Score

Market Demand (0-100)
Competition Level (0-100)
Consumer Awareness (0-100)
Market Readiness Score

71

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

The Market Readiness Score measures how prepared the market is for the launch of a new product, considering demand, competition, and awareness.

Market readiness helps businesses assess if the market is prepared for their product, aiding in optimizing launch strategies and minimizing risks.
