Competitive Positioning Strength Score

This calculator helps evaluate your competitive positioning in the market based on key factors like brand awareness, product differentiation, and customer loyalty.

Formula:

The score is calculated by averaging the following factors:

  • Brand Awareness: Score between 1 (low) and 10 (high).
  • Product Differentiation: Score between 1 (low) and 10 (high).
  • Customer Loyalty: Score between 1 (low) and 10 (high).

The formula is:

  • Competitive Positioning Strength Score = (Brand Awareness + Product Differentiation + Customer Loyalty) / 3
Example Calculation:

If the following data is provided:

  • Brand Awareness: 8
  • Product Differentiation: 7
  • Customer Loyalty: 9

The score would be:

Competitive Positioning Strength Score = (8 + 7 + 9) / 3 = 8.0
