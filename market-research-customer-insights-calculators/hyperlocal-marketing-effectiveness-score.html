Hyperlocal Marketing Effectiveness Score

This calculator helps evaluate the effectiveness of your hyperlocal marketing campaigns based on different parameters.

Formula:

The formula for calculating the Hyperlocal Marketing Effectiveness Score (HMES) is:

            HMES = (Local Engagement * 0.4) + (Brand Recognition * 0.3) + (Customer Retention * 0.3)
Example Calculation:

If the values for local engagement, brand recognition, and customer retention are as follows:

  • Local Engagement: 80
  • Brand Recognition: 70
  • Customer Retention: 75

The formula will be:

            HMES = (80 * 0.4) + (70 * 0.3) + (75 * 0.3) = 32 + 21 + 22.5 = 75.5

Input for Hyperlocal Marketing Effectiveness Score Calculation

Local Engagement (0-100)
Brand Recognition (0-100)
Customer Retention (0-100)
