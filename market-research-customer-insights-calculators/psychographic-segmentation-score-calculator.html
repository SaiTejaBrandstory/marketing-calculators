Psychographic Segmentation Score Calculator

This calculator helps you evaluate the Psychographic Segmentation Score based on factors like interests, lifestyle, and personality traits.

Formula:

The Psychographic Segmentation Score is calculated using the following formula:

  • Psychographic Segmentation Score = (Interest Score * Lifestyle Score * Personality Traits Score) / 1000
Example Calculation:

If the following data is provided:

  • Interest Score: 80
  • Lifestyle Score: 70
  • Personality Traits Score: 60

The Psychographic Segmentation Score would be:

Psychographic Segmentation Score = (80 * 70 * 60) / 1000 = 336
