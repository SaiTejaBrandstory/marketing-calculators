Personalized Marketing vs. Mass Marketing ROI Comparison

This calculator compares the ROI of personalized marketing versus mass marketing based on various input values.

Formula:

The ROI comparison is calculated as follows:

  • ROI = (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100
Example Calculation:

For Personalized Marketing:

  • Revenue: ₹50,000
  • Cost: ₹20,000
  • ROI = (50,000 - 20,000) / 20,000 × 100 = 150%

For Mass Marketing:

  • Revenue: ₹80,000
  • Cost: ₹30,000
  • ROI = (80,000 - 30,000) / 30,000 × 100 = 166.67%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

ROI (Return on Investment) in marketing measures the effectiveness of a marketing campaign by comparing the revenue generated to the cost incurred.

The formula for calculating ROI is: (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100.

Personalized marketing often generates higher revenue relative to its cost, as it targets specific segments of customers with tailored messages, leading to higher engagement and conversions.
