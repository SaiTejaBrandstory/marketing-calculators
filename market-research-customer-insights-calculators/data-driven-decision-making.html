Data-Driven Decision-Making Effectiveness Score

This calculator helps measure how effectively your organization uses data for decision-making.

Formula:

The effectiveness score is calculated as:

  • Effectiveness Score = ( (Data Quality Score × 0.3) + (Data Utilization Score × 0.3) + (Decision Impact Score × 0.4) )
Example Calculation:

For the following data:

  • Data Quality Score: 80
  • Data Utilization Score: 70
  • Decision Impact Score: 85

Effectiveness Score:

( (80 × 0.3) + (70 × 0.3) + (85 × 0.4) ) = 79

Input for Data-Driven Decision-Making Effectiveness

Data Quality Score (0-100)
Data Utilization Score (0-100)
Decision Impact Score (0-100)
