Customer Journey Mapping Effectiveness Score

This tool helps evaluate the effectiveness of your customer journey mapping process by assessing customer touchpoints and their impact on the overall customer experience.

Formula:

Customer Journey Effectiveness Score = (Touchpoint Optimization + Customer Engagement + Brand Consistency + Conversion Rate) / 4

Example Calculation:

Touchpoint Optimization: 80

Customer Engagement: 75

Brand Consistency: 85

Conversion Rate: 90

Customer Journey Effectiveness Score = (80 + 75 + 85 + 90) / 4 = 82.5
