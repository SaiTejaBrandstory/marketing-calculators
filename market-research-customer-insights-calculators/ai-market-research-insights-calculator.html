Formula:

The AI-Powered Market Research Insights Calculator considers the following key metrics:

  • Customer Demand Score: A score based on customer demand for the product/service.
  • Market Trend Score: A score indicating how the market is shifting in favor or against the product.
  • Competitor Position Score: A score indicating how your product performs compared to competitors.

The formula to calculate the AI Insights Score is:

AI Insights Score = (Customer Demand Score + Market Trend Score + Competitor Position Score) / 3

Example Calculation:

If you have the following scores:

  • Customer Demand Score: 8
  • Market Trend Score: 7
  • Competitor Position Score: 6

The AI Insights Score is calculated as: (8 + 7 + 6) / 3 = 7.0.
