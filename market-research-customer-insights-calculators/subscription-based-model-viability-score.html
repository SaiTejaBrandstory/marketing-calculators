Subscription-Based Model Viability Score

This calculator helps you evaluate the viability of a subscription-based business model.

Formula:

The score is calculated using the following formula:

Viability Score = (Churn Rate × 0.4) + (Lifetime Value × 0.3) + (Conversion Rate × 0.3)

Example Calculation:

For example, if the values are:

  • Churn Rate: 10%
  • Lifetime Value: ₹5000
  • Conversion Rate: 5%

The Viability Score would be: (10 × 0.4) + (5000 × 0.3) + (5 × 0.3) = 4 + 1500 + 1.5 = 1505.5
