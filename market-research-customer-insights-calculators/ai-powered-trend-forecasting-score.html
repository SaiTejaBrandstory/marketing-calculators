AI-Powered Trend Forecasting Score

This calculator estimates the AI-powered trend forecasting score based on historical data accuracy, prediction confidence, model complexity, and market trends impact.

Formula:

The Trend Forecasting Score is calculated as a weighted average of the following factors:

  • Historical Data Accuracy (40% weight)
  • Prediction Confidence (30% weight)
  • Model Complexity (20% weight)
  • Market Trends Impact (10% weight)
Example Calculation:

If the following data is provided:

  • Historical Data Accuracy: 90%
  • Prediction Confidence: 85%
  • Model Complexity: 80%
  • Market Trends Impact: 70%

The AI-Powered Trend Forecasting Score would be:

Trend Forecasting Score = (90 × 0.4 + 85 × 0.3 + 80 × 0.2 + 70 × 0.1) = 84.50
