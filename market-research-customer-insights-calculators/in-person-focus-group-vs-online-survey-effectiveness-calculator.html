In-Person Focus Group vs. Online Survey Effectiveness Calculator

This calculator compares the effectiveness of in-person focus groups versus online surveys based on revenue and costs.

Formula:

The effectiveness comparison is calculated as follows:

  • Effectiveness = (Revenue / Cost) × 100
Example Calculation:

For In-Person Focus Group:

  • Revenue: ₹30,000
  • Cost: ₹10,000
  • Effectiveness = (30,000 / 10,000) × 100 = 300%

For Online Survey:

  • Revenue: ₹25,000
  • Cost: ₹5,000
  • Effectiveness = (25,000 / 5,000) × 100 = 500%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

In-person focus groups allow for deeper engagement and discussions, providing richer qualitative data, though they often come with higher costs compared to online surveys.

The effectiveness of online surveys is measured by comparing the revenue generated from the survey insights versus the cost incurred for running the survey.

It depends on the specific context of the research, budget, and the depth of insights needed. In-person focus groups tend to provide richer, qualitative insights, whereas online surveys are more cost-effective and easier to scale.
