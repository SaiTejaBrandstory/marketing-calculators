SERP Click-Through Rate (CTR) Calculator

The SERP Click-Through Rate (CTR) Calculator helps you determine the effectiveness of your website's listing in the search engine results pages (SERP). By inputting the number of impressions and the number of clicks, you can calculate the click-through rate (CTR) to evaluate your organic search performance.

This tool calculates the percentage of users who clicked on your link after seeing it in the SERP. A higher CTR indicates that your title and meta description are compelling, while a lower CTR suggests room for optimization.

Formula:

CTR = (Clicks / Impressions) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Clicks: 250
  • Impressions: 5000
  • CTR: (250 / 5000) × 100 = 5.00%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Click-Through Rate (CTR) is a metric that measures the percentage of users who click on a link after seeing it in the search engine results page (SERP). It is calculated by dividing the number of clicks by the number of impressions and multiplying by 100 to get the percentage.

To improve your CTR, optimize your meta title and description, use compelling language, include targeted keywords, and ensure that your listing stands out in the SERP.
