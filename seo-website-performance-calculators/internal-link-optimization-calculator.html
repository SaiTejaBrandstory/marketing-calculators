Internal Link Optimization Calculator

The Internal Link Optimization Calculator helps measure how well your internal linking structure distributes link equity and improves SEO. Optimized internal linking enhances page authority, user experience, and search engine indexing.

Formula:

Optimization Score = (Total Internal Links / Total Pages) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Total Internal Links: 300
  • Total Pages: 50
  • Optimization Score: (300 / 50) × 100 = 600%
