Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Keyword clustering is a technique in SEO where related keywords are grouped into clusters to improve the relevance of content and optimize for a wider range of search queries. It helps search engines understand the content better and improves rankings.

By grouping related keywords into clusters, you can create content that covers multiple relevant terms, increasing topical relevance and enhancing the chances of ranking for related search queries. This strategy also helps to reduce keyword cannibalization.
