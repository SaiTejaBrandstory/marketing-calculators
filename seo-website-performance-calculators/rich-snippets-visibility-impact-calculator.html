FAQ

A rich snippet is a search result with additional data beyond the title, URL, and description. This can include ratings, reviews, pricing, product details, and more.

Rich snippets can improve visibility in search results by making your listing stand out with additional information. This can lead to higher click-through rates (CTR) and improve your SEO performance.

Rich snippets are implemented using structured data markup (such as JSON-LD, Microdata, or RDFa). This markup provides search engines with additional information about your content, allowing it to be displayed as a rich snippet.
