Content Decay Impact Calculator

The Content Decay Impact Calculator helps estimate traffic loss due to outdated content. Search rankings often decline over time, leading to a drop in organic visitors.

Updating and repurposing old content can help regain rankings and maintain steady traffic flow. This calculator provides an estimate of the potential traffic loss over time.

Formula:

Traffic Loss (%) = (Initial Traffic - Current Traffic) / Initial Traffic × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Initial Traffic: 10,000
  • Current Traffic: 7,000
  • Traffic Loss: ((10,000 - 7,000) / 10,000) × 100 = 30%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Content decay refers to the gradual decline in organic search traffic due to aging content, outdated information, and changes in search engine algorithms.

You can prevent content decay by updating old posts, adding fresh insights, improving SEO, and repurposing content across different channels.

It is recommended to refresh your content every 6-12 months, depending on industry trends and keyword performance.
