FAQ

This calculator helps evaluate the risk of having duplicate content across your website. It helps determine the potential SEO impact caused by duplicate content and provides a risk score based on the number of duplicate pages on the site.

Duplicate content can confuse search engines, making it difficult for them to determine which version of a page to index and rank. This can lead to lower rankings, reduced visibility, and even penalties from search engines.

To reduce duplicate content, consider using canonical tags, removing unnecessary duplicate pages, using redirects, and ensuring that each page has unique content to prevent search engines from indexing multiple versions of the same content.
