SEO ROI Calculator

The SEO ROI Calculator helps you assess the return on investment from your SEO efforts in Indian Rupees (₹). It compares the cost of your SEO campaign with the additional revenue generated from organic search traffic, giving you an idea of how effective your SEO strategy is.

By inputting the monthly cost of your SEO campaign, the increase in organic traffic, and your average conversion rate, this tool calculates the ROI and helps you decide whether your SEO efforts are profitable.

Formula:

ROI = (Revenue from SEO - SEO Campaign Cost) / SEO Campaign Cost × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Revenue from SEO: ₹10,000
  • SEO Campaign Cost: ₹2,000
  • ROI: (₹10,000 - ₹2,000) / ₹2,000 × 100 = 400%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

ROI in SEO refers to the return on investment from your SEO campaigns. It is calculated by comparing the revenue generated from increased organic search traffic to the cost of the SEO campaign. A positive ROI indicates that your SEO efforts are profitable.

To improve your SEO ROI, focus on optimizing high-converting pages, targeting relevant keywords, improving page load times, and building high-quality backlinks. Regularly monitor and adjust your SEO strategies for better results.
