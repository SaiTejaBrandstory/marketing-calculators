FAQ

This calculator helps estimate the SEO boost you can expect by switching your website from HTTP to HTTPS. By moving to HTTPS, you signal trustworthiness to both users and search engines, which can positively impact SEO rankings.

HTTPS is considered a ranking factor by search engines like Google. It indicates a secure connection, which improves trust and safety for users. Websites using HTTPS are prioritized in search rankings, which can lead to a boost in visibility.

To switch to HTTPS, you need to obtain an SSL certificate for your domain. Once the certificate is installed on your web server, you can update your website's URLs to use "https://" instead of "http://", ensuring all traffic is redirected to the secure version of your site.
