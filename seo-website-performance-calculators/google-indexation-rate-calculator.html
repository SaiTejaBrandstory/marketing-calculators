Google Indexation Rate Calculator

The Google Indexation Rate Calculator helps determine the percentage of your website's pages that are indexed by Google. A higher indexation rate means more of your site is discoverable in search results, improving your SEO performance.

Formula:

Indexation Rate = (Indexed Pages / Total Pages) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Indexed Pages: 450
  • Total Pages: 500
  • Indexation Rate: (450 / 500) × 100 = 90%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Indexation rate refers to the percentage of your website's pages that have been indexed by Google. The more pages indexed, the better your website’s visibility in search results.

To improve your indexation rate, you should ensure that your site is crawlable by Google, submit an XML sitemap, and optimize your site’s internal linking structure. Additionally, fix any crawling errors in Google Search Console.
