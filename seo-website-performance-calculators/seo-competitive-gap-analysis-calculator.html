SEO Competitive Gap Analysis Calculator

The SEO Competitive Gap Analysis Calculator helps you evaluate your website's SEO performance against competitors. By comparing keyword rankings and traffic data, you can identify opportunities to improve your site's visibility and traffic.

With this tool, you can get insights into how well your website is ranking for important keywords compared to your competitors. This helps you target gaps and areas for improvement in your SEO strategy.

Formula:

SEO Gap = |(Your Rank - Competitor's Rank) × Traffic Potential|

Example Calculation:
  • Your Rank: 5
  • Competitor's Rank: 8
  • Traffic Potential: 2000
  • SEO Gap: |(5 - 8) × 2000| = 6000

An SEO Competitive Gap Analysis evaluates your website's SEO performance in comparison to your competitors, helping you identify areas where they may be outperforming you in search engine rankings and traffic potential.
