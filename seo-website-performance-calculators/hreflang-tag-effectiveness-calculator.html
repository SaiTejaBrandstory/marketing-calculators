Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Hreflang tags are HTML attributes used to tell search engines which language and region a webpage is intended for. They help ensure the correct version of a webpage is shown to users in different regions and languages, improving both user experience and SEO rankings.

To ensure that your Hreflang tags are effective, make sure they are implemented correctly on all pages, include the proper language and region codes, and avoid errors like self-referencing tags. Regularly validate your Hreflang tags to ensure they are working as intended.
