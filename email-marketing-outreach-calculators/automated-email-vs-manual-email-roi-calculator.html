Automated Email vs. Manual Email ROI Calculator

This calculator compares the ROI of Automated vs. Manual Email campaigns. By calculating the cost and revenue from both approaches, you can determine which method offers a higher return on investment.

Formula for ROI:

ROI (%) = ((Revenue - Cost) / Cost) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Revenue: $1,500
  • Cost: $300
  • ROI: ((1,500 - 300) / 300) × 100 = 400.00%

ROI for automated email campaigns measures the revenue generated against the cost of setting up and sending the automated emails. It helps understand the efficiency of the automated process.

ROI is calculated using the formula: ROI = ((Revenue - Cost) / Cost) × 100. It is a measure of the profitability of the email campaign, either automated or manual.
