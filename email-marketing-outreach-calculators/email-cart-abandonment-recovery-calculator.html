Email Cart Abandonment Recovery Calculator (INR)

The Email Cart Abandonment Recovery Calculator helps you estimate how effective your email recovery campaigns are in recovering abandoned carts. By inputting your cart abandonment rate, recovery rate, and average order value in Indian Rupees (₹), this calculator will show you the potential revenue recovery.

Formula for Recovery Revenue:

Recovery Revenue = (Abandoned Cart Value × Recovery Rate) × Average Order Value

Example Calculation:
  • Abandoned Cart Value: ₹5,00,000
  • Recovery Rate: 20%
  • Average Order Value: ₹15,000
  • Recovery Revenue: (5,00,000 × 0.20) × 15,000 = ₹1,50,00,000

The cart abandonment recovery rate is the percentage of customers who return to their cart and complete the purchase after receiving a cart recovery email.

The recovery revenue is calculated using the formula: (Abandoned Cart Value × Recovery Rate) × Average Order Value. This gives you the total revenue from recovered carts.

To improve your recovery rate, ensure that your cart abandonment emails are personalized, timely, and include strong call-to-actions or incentives like discounts to encourage the customer to complete the purchase.
