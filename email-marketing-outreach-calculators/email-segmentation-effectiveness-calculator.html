Email Segmentation Effectiveness Calculator

The Email Segmentation Effectiveness Calculator helps measure how effective your email segmentation strategy is by comparing the open and click-through rates of segmented vs. non-segmented email lists.

Formula:

Effectiveness Score (%) = (Segmented Open Rate - Non-Segmented Open Rate) / Non-Segmented Open Rate × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Segmented Open Rate: 25%
  • Non-Segmented Open Rate: 15%
  • Effectiveness Score: ((25 - 15) / 15) × 100 = 66.67%

Email segmentation is the process of dividing an email list into smaller segments based on criteria such as demographics, purchase history, or engagement levels. This helps tailor content to specific groups for better results.

Segmentation allows you to send more personalized emails, increasing relevance and engagement. This typically results in higher open and click-through rates, improving overall campaign performance.

The Effectiveness Score measures how much better the segmented email open rate is compared to the non-segmented email open rate, expressed as a percentage.
