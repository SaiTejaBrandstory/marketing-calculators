Abandoned Cart Email Follow-Up Efficiency Calculator

This calculator helps you measure the effectiveness of your abandoned cart email follow-up campaigns by estimating the recovery rate based on different factors such as email open rate, click rate, and conversion rate.

Formula:

Follow-Up Efficiency (%) = (Open Rate + Click Rate + Conversion Rate) / 3

Example Calculation:
  • Open Rate: 40%
  • Click Rate: 15%
  • Conversion Rate: 5%
  • Follow-Up Efficiency: (40 + 15 + 5) / 3 = 20%

Follow-Up Efficiency measures how effective your abandoned cart email follow-up campaign is in recovering abandoned sales. It is calculated using key email metrics like open rate, click rate, and conversion rate.
