Welcome Email Conversion Rate Calculator

The Welcome Email Conversion Rate Calculator helps you determine how effective your welcome emails are at converting new users. This metric is important for assessing how well your initial email engagement translates into desired actions.

Formula for Conversion Rate:

Conversion Rate (%) = (Conversions / Emails Delivered) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Emails Delivered: 500
  • Conversions: 50
  • Conversion Rate: (50 / 500) × 100 = 10.00%
