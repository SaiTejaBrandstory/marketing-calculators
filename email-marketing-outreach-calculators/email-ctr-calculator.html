Email Click-Through Rate (CTR) Calculator

The Email CTR Calculator helps measure how many recipients clicked on a link in your email compared to the total emails delivered. A higher CTR indicates better engagement and compelling email content.

Formula:

CTR (%) = (Clicks / Emails Delivered) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Clicks: 200
  • Emails Delivered: 2000
  • CTR: (200 / 2000) × 100 = 10%

FAQ

Click-Through Rate (CTR) is the percentage of email recipients who clicked on a link within your email. It measures the effectiveness of email campaigns in driving user engagement.

A good email CTR varies by industry, but an average range is 2-5%. Higher CTRs can be achieved by improving email design, CTA placement, and audience segmentation.

To improve your email CTR, consider:
  • Writing compelling CTAs (Call-To-Action)
  • Using engaging subject lines
  • Personalizing email content
  • Segmenting your audience for relevance
  • Optimizing email design for mobile
