Brand Loyalty Impact via Email Campaigns

This calculator helps you estimate the impact of email campaigns on your brand's loyalty by calculating customer retention and purchase frequency based on email interactions.

Formula:

Brand Loyalty Impact (%) = (Increased Purchases / Total Customers) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Increased Purchases: 200
  • Total Customers: 1000
  • Brand Loyalty Impact: (200 / 1000) × 100 = 20%
