Cold Email Conversion Rate Calculator

The Cold Email Conversion Rate Calculator helps estimate the effectiveness of your cold email campaigns. This tool calculates the percentage of email recipients who take the desired action, based on the number of successful conversions and the number of emails sent.

Formula:

Conversion Rate (%) = (Number of Conversions / Number of Emails Sent) × 100

  • Number of Emails Sent: 500

The Cold Email Conversion Rate is the percentage of recipients who take the desired action (e.g., clicking a link, making a purchase) after receiving a cold email.

The Conversion Rate is calculated by dividing the number of conversions (successful actions) by the number of emails sent, then multiplying by 100 to get a percentage. (Conversions / Emails Sent) × 100

The conversion rate can be affected by the quality of your email content, subject line, targeting, timing, and the relevance of your offer.

A good conversion rate depends on the industry, but typically, a conversion rate of 1-5% is considered average for cold emails. Higher rates can indicate better targeting and engagement.
