Email List Growth Rate Calculator

The Email List Growth Rate Calculator helps you determine the rate at which your email list is growing. By measuring how quickly your email subscriber list expands, you can evaluate the effectiveness of your email campaigns.

Formula:

Growth Rate (%) = ((New Subscribers - Old Subscribers) / Old Subscribers) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Old Subscribers: 500
  • New Subscribers: 600
  • Growth Rate: ((600 - 500) / 500) × 100 = 20%
