Mobile vs. Desktop Email Open Rate Calculator

The Mobile vs. Desktop Email Open Rate Calculator helps you compare the open rates of your email campaigns on mobile devices versus desktop. Input your open rates for both devices to understand the difference and optimize for better email engagement.

Formula for Open Rate Comparison:

Open Rate Comparison = (Mobile Open Rate / Desktop Open Rate) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Mobile Open Rate: 25%
  • Desktop Open Rate: 20%
  • Open Rate Comparison: (25 / 20) × 100 = 125%

An email open rate is the percentage of recipients who open a given email compared to the total number of recipients who received it.

Understanding the differences between mobile and desktop email open rates helps you tailor your campaigns for specific devices, leading to better engagement and conversions.

To improve mobile email open rates, optimize your subject lines, use responsive email designs, and ensure your content is mobile-friendly.
