B2B vs. B2C Email Marketing Performance Estimator

This tool helps estimate the performance of email marketing campaigns for both B2B and B2C audiences, based on engagement rates such as open rates, click-through rates (CTR), and conversion rates.

Formula:

Estimated Performance (%) = (Open Rate + Click-Through Rate + Conversion Rate) / 3

Example Calculation:
  • Open Rate (B2B): 25%
  • Click-Through Rate (B2B): 15%
  • Conversion Rate (B2B): 5%
  • Estimated Performance (B2B): (25 + 15 + 5) / 3 = 15%
  • Open Rate (B2C): 40%
  • Click-Through Rate (B2C): 25%
  • Conversion Rate (B2C): 10%
  • Estimated Performance (B2C): (40 + 25 + 10) / 3 = 25%

FAQ

Email marketing performance is measured based on open rates, click-through rates, and conversion rates. These metrics help you determine how well your email marketing campaign is performing with your audience.

B2B email marketing is targeted toward businesses and focuses on building professional relationships, while B2C email marketing is focused on engaging individual consumers to drive sales or conversions.
