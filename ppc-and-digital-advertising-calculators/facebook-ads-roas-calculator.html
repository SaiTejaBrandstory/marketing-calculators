Facebook Ads ROAS Calculator

Calculate the Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) for your Facebook Ads campaigns and optimize your budget.

Formula:

ROAS = Total Revenue / Ad Spend

Example Calculation:

Suppose you spend ₹10,000 on Facebook Ads and generate ₹50,000 in revenue.

ROAS = 50,000 / 10,000 = 5

Your ROAS is 5, meaning you earn ₹5 for every ₹1 spent.
