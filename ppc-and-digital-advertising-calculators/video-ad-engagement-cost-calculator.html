Video Ad Engagement Cost Calculator

Calculate the cost per engagement for your video ads to measure the efficiency of your campaigns.

Formula:

Engagement Cost = Total Ad Spend / Total Engagements

Example Calculation:

Suppose you spent ₹5000 on video ads, and you received 2000 engagements.

Engagement Cost = 5000 / 2000 = ₹2.50 per engagement
