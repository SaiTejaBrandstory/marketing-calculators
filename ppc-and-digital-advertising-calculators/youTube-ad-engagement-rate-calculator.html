YouTube Ad Engagement Rate Calculator

Calculate the engagement rate for your YouTube ad campaigns based on views, likes, comments, and shares.

Formula:

Engagement Rate (%) = ((Likes + Comments + Shares) / Views) × 100

Example Calculation:

Suppose your video has 10,000 views, 500 likes, 200 comments, and 300 shares.

Engagement Rate = ((500 + 200 + 300) / 10,000) × 100 = 10%
