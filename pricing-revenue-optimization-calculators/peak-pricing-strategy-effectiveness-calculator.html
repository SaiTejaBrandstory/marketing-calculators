Peak Pricing Strategy Effectiveness Calculator

This tool helps you calculate the effectiveness of your peak pricing strategy by comparing the original price with the peak price during high-demand periods. It calculates how much revenue you gain with peak pricing.

Formula:

Effectiveness = (Peak Price - Original Price) / Original Price * 100

Example Calculation:

Original Price: ₹1000

Peak Price: ₹1200

Effectiveness = (1200 - 1000) / 1000 * 100 = 20%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Peak pricing is the practice of increasing prices during high-demand periods, such as holidays, weekends, or peak hours.

Peak pricing can significantly increase revenue during periods of high demand, allowing businesses to capitalize on customers' willingness to pay more.
