Pay-What-You-Want (PWYW) Pricing Model Feasibility Score

This calculator helps you assess the feasibility of a Pay-What-You-Want (PWYW) pricing model. It calculates the feasibility score based on factors like average price offered by customers, the desired target price, and the standard cost of the product or service.

Formula:

Feasibility Score = (Average Price Offered / Target Price) * (1 - Standard Cost Ratio)

Example Calculation:

Average Price Offered: ₹250

Target Price: ₹300

Standard Cost Ratio: 0.5

Feasibility Score = (250 / 300) * (1 - 0.5) = 0.4167

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Pay-What-You-Want (PWYW) pricing is a pricing strategy where customers are given the freedom to decide how much they want to pay for a product or service.

The Feasibility Score helps assess the viability of a PWYW model. It's calculated by comparing the average price offered by customers to the target price, adjusted by the standard cost ratio.
