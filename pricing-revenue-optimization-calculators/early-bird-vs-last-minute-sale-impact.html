Base Price: ₹1000

Discount Rate: 0.20 (20%)

Number of Sales: 150

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

An Early Bird Discount is a promotional offer where customers receive a discount for purchasing in advance, usually before the event or product is released.

A Last-Minute Sale is a promotion where customers can purchase a product at a discount just before the product or service is unavailable, usually as a last opportunity to buy.
