Early Access vs. General Launch Pricing Impact Calculator

This tool helps calculate the revenue impact of early access pricing versus general launch pricing. By comparing the sales and pricing during early access and the general launch phase, you can assess how the pricing strategy affects overall revenue.

Formula:

Revenue Impact = (Early Access Price * Early Access Sales) + (Launch Price * Launch Sales) - Total Revenue

Example Calculation:

Early Access Price: ₹200

Early Access Sales: 500

Launch Price: ₹350

Launch Sales: 1000

Total Revenue: ₹200,000

Revenue Impact = (200 * 500) + (350 * 1000) - 200000 = ₹250,000

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Early access pricing is the special pricing offered to customers before a product is fully launched, often at a discounted rate in exchange for early feedback or support.

Comparing early access and general launch pricing helps you assess the revenue and customer response to different pricing strategies and decide which one yields the best results.
