Pay-Per-Use Model Profitability Calculator

This tool helps calculate the profitability of the Pay-Per-Use pricing model based on usage fee, customer acquisition cost (CAC), and usage volume.

Formula:

Profitability = (Usage Fee * Usage Volume) - CAC

Example Calculation:

Usage Fee: ₹100

Usage Volume: 500 units

CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost): ₹2000

Profitability = (100 * 500) - 2000 = ₹48,000

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

In a Pay-Per-Use pricing model, customers pay based on their usage of the service or product. It is ideal for businesses that want to charge customers according to how much they consume.

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) is important because it determines the cost of acquiring a customer and helps to assess whether the revenue generated by usage is enough to cover the CAC.
