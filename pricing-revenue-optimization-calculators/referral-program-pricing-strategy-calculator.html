Referral Program Pricing Strategy Calculator

This calculator helps you assess the impact of a referral program on your pricing strategy. It calculates the potential revenue from a referral program based on referral discount, the number of referrals, and customer lifetime value (CLV).

Formula:

Revenue from Referrals = (Number of Referrals * Referral Discount) * CLV

Example Calculation:

Referral Discount: ₹50

Number of Referrals: 20

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): ₹1000

Revenue from Referrals = (20 * 50) * 1000 = ₹1,000,000

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

A referral program pricing strategy is a marketing approach where you offer discounts or incentives to customers who refer new customers to your business.

The revenue from referrals is calculated by multiplying the number of referrals, the referral discount, and the customer lifetime value (CLV).
