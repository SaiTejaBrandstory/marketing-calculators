Cart Abandonment Discount Strategy ROI Calculator

This tool calculates the Return on Investment (ROI) for a Cart Abandonment Discount Strategy, considering the discount percentage, the abandonment rate, and the conversion rate after applying the discount.

Formula:

ROI = (Revenue from Conversions - Discount Cost) / Discount Cost

Example Calculation:

Revenue from Conversions: ₹20,000

Discount Cost: ₹2,000

ROI = (20,000 - 2,000) / 2,000 = 9

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Cart abandonment occurs when a customer adds items to their online shopping cart but leaves the site without completing the purchase.

ROI helps measure the effectiveness of a discount strategy by comparing the revenue generated through conversions to the cost of offering the discounts.
