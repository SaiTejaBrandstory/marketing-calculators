One-Time Purchase vs Subscription Model Revenue Forecasting

This tool helps you compare the revenue generated by a One-Time Purchase model vs a Subscription model over a defined period of time.

Formula:

One-Time Purchase Revenue = Price per Unit * Number of Units Sold

Subscription Revenue = Subscription Fee * Number of Subscribers * Duration

Example Calculation:

Price per Unit (One-Time Purchase): ₹500

Number of Units Sold: 200

One-Time Purchase Revenue = 500 * 200 = ₹100,000

Subscription Fee: ₹500

Number of Subscribers: 100

Duration (Months): 12

Subscription Revenue = 500 * 100 * 12 = ₹600,000

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

A one-time purchase model generates revenue through single payments made by customers for each unit or product sold.

A subscription model generates recurring revenue by charging customers a regular fee (monthly, yearly) for ongoing access to a product or service.
