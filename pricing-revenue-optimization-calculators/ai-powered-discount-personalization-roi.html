AI-Powered Discount Personalization ROI Estimator

This tool estimates the return on investment (ROI) of personalized discounts powered by AI. It helps assess how AI-driven discount strategies impact overall revenue and customer retention.

Formula:

ROI = (Revenue from Personalized Discounts - Cost of Personalization) / Cost of Personalization

Example Calculation:

Revenue from Personalized Discounts: ₹100,000

Cost of Personalization: ₹20,000

ROI = (100,000 - 20,000) / 20,000 = ₹4
