Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) to Revenue Ratio Calculator

This tool helps you calculate the Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) to Revenue Ratio, allowing you to measure the efficiency of your customer acquisition efforts in relation to revenue generated.

Formula:

CPA to Revenue Ratio = (Customer Acquisition Cost / Revenue) * 100

Example Calculation:

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): ₹200

Revenue: ₹1000

CPA to Revenue Ratio = (200 / 1000) * 100 = 20%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) is the cost of acquiring a new customer, including all marketing and sales expenses.

The CPA to Revenue Ratio helps businesses evaluate whether their customer acquisition costs are proportional to the revenue generated. A high ratio may indicate inefficiencies.
