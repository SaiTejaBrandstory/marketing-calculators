Personalized Pricing Strategy ROI Calculator

This tool helps calculate the ROI of your personalized pricing strategy by considering the personalized price increase, number of customers impacted, and the increase in revenue.

Formula:

ROI = (Revenue from Personalized Pricing - Cost of Personalization) / Cost of Personalization

Example Calculation:

Revenue from Personalized Pricing: ₹500,000

Cost of Personalization: ₹100,000

ROI = (500,000 - 100,000) / 100,000 = 4.00

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

A personalized pricing strategy involves tailoring prices for individual customers or segments based on their willingness to pay, behavior, and purchase history.
