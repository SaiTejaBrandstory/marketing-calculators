Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

A volume-based discount is a price reduction offered to customers based on the quantity of products purchased. It encourages larger purchases by providing a better deal for bulk buys.

The optimization score helps businesses determine the effectiveness of their volume discount strategy by considering how much discount is applied and the resulting impact on overall revenue.
