LTV:CAC Ratio Calculator

This tool helps you calculate the Lifetime Value to Customer Acquisition Cost ratio, which is an important metric for assessing the profitability and efficiency of your marketing efforts.

Formula:

LTV:CAC Ratio = Lifetime Value (LTV) / Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

Example Calculation:

Lifetime Value (LTV): ₹150,000

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): ₹50,000

LTV:CAC Ratio = 150,000 / 50,000 = 3

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

The LTV:CAC Ratio compares the lifetime value (LTV) of a customer to the cost of acquiring that customer (CAC). A higher ratio indicates better customer retention and acquisition efficiency.

The formula is: LTV:CAC Ratio = Lifetime Value (LTV) / Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC).

A high LTV:CAC ratio shows that the business is generating significant value from its customers compared to what it spends on acquiring them, indicating strong profitability and sustainable growth.
