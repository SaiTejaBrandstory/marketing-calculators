Flash Sale Impact on Long-Term Revenue

This tool helps calculate the impact of a flash sale on your long-term revenue by factoring in the discount percentage, sale duration, customer retention rate, and average lifetime value (LTV) of customers.

Formula:

Long-Term Revenue = (Flash Sale Revenue) + (Post-Sale Retained Revenue)

Example Calculation:

Flash Sale Revenue: ₹50,000

Post-Sale Retained Revenue (due to 20% retention rate): ₹10,000

Long-Term Revenue = ₹50,000 + ₹10,000 = ₹60,000
