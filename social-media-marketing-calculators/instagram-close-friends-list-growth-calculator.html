Instagram Close Friends List Growth Calculator

The Instagram Close Friends List Growth Calculator helps track the growth of your Close Friends List over a given period. It calculates the percentage growth based on the number of new people added and the original list size.

This tool helps you assess how much your Close Friends List is expanding, giving insights into engagement and exclusivity.

Formula:

Growth Percentage = ((New Additions / Original List Size) × 100)

Example Calculation:
  • Original List Size: 100
  • New Additions: 25
