Content Repurposing ROI Calculator

The Content Repurposing ROI Calculator helps you calculate the return on investment (ROI) for repurposing content across various platforms. By measuring engagement and reach, this tool helps you determine the effectiveness of repurposing your content.

Repurposing content allows you to maximize the value of your original work by expanding its reach and engagement with minimal effort.

Formula:

ROI = (Total Revenue from Repurposed Content - Total Cost of Repurposing) / Total Cost of Repurposing × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Total Revenue from Repurposed Content: ₹15,000
  • Total Cost of Repurposing: ₹5,000
  • ROI: (15,000 - 5,000) / 5,000 × 100 = 200%

Frequently Asked Questions

Content repurposing involves taking existing content and adapting it for use on different platforms or in different formats.

To calculate ROI, subtract the total cost of repurposing from the total revenue generated by the content and divide by the cost, then multiply by 100.

A good ROI for content repurposing is typically anything above 100%. This indicates that the repurposed content is generating more revenue than the cost to repurpose it.
