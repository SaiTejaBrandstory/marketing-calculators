Formula:

Thought Leadership Score = ((Likes × 1) + (Comments × 2) + (Shares × 3) + (Followers × 0.5)) / Total Posts

Example Calculation:
  • Likes: 500
  • Comments: 200
  • Shares: 100
  • Followers: 10,000
  • Total Posts: 50
  • Score: ((500 × 1 + 200 × 2 + 100 × 3 + 10,000 × 0.5) / 50) = 124
