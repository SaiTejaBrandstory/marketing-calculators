Instagram Paid Partnership Performance Calculator

The Instagram Paid Partnership Performance Calculator helps you determine the effectiveness of your Instagram paid collaborations by comparing the cost of partnership with the expected returns. By entering key metrics such as the number of posts, engagement rate, cost per post, and expected conversions, you can assess the return on investment (ROI) for your Instagram campaigns.

This calculator evaluates the revenue generated by Instagram posts in a paid partnership. The ROI formula compares the total revenue expected from the campaign with the cost of collaboration to help you understand the profitability of each campaign.

Formula:

ROI = ((Revenue - Cost) / Cost) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Revenue from Instagram Posts: 5000
  • Cost of Paid Partnership: 2000
  • ROI: ((5000 - 2000) / 2000) × 100 = 150%
