Facebook Ad Placement Efficiency Calculator

The Facebook Ad Placement Efficiency Calculator helps advertisers evaluate the performance of different ad placements by comparing cost and conversion rates. This tool enables businesses to optimize ad spend and maximize ROI.

By analyzing the cost per conversion for various placements, advertisers can make data-driven decisions to allocate their budget more effectively and improve ad performance.

Formula:

Efficiency Score = (Conversions / Ad Spend) × 100

Example Calculation:
  • Ad Spend: ₹50,000
  • Conversions: 1,250
  • Efficiency Score: (1,250 / 50,000) × 100 = 2.5%
