Formula:

Engagement Growth Score = ((Likes × 1) + (Comments × 2) + (Shares × 3) + (Followers × 0.5)) / Total Posts

Example Calculation:
  • Likes: 300
  • Comments: 150
  • Shares: 50
  • Followers: 8,000
  • Total Posts: 40
  • Score: ((300 × 1 + 150 × 2 + 50 × 3 + 8,000 × 0.5) / 40) = 118.75
