Branded Content Performance Score

Formula:

The formula for calculating the branded content performance score:

Content Score = (Engagements × 0.4) + (Reach × 0.3) + (Conversions × 0.3)

Example Calculation:

If:

  • Engagements = 500
  • Reach = 1000
  • Conversions = 200

Content Score = (500 × 0.4) + (1000 × 0.3) + (200 × 0.3) = 200 + 300 + 60 = 560

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

The Branded Content Performance Score is a metric used to measure the effectiveness of branded content, based on engagement, reach, and conversions.

Focus on creating content that resonates with your audience to drive higher engagement, reach more people, and increase conversions through effective calls to action.

A good branded content score typically ranges from 500 to 800, but this can vary depending on your industry, audience size, and campaign goals.
