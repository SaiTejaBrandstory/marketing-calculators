Affiliate Marketing Performance Predictor

Formula:

The formula for predicting affiliate marketing performance:

Predicted Revenue = (Clicks × Conversion Rate × Avg. Order Value) - Cost

Example Calculation:

If:

  • Clicks = 10,000
  • Conversion Rate = 2% (0.02)
  • Avg. Order Value = ₹1,500
  • Cost = ₹50,000

Predicted Revenue = (10,000 × 0.02 × 1,500) - 50,000 = ₹250,000
