Marketing Efficiency Ratio Calculator

Formula:

The formula for calculating the marketing efficiency ratio:

Marketing Efficiency Ratio = (Revenue from Marketing Activities) / (Cost of Marketing Activities)

Example Calculation:

For example, if:

  • Revenue from Marketing Activities = 100,000
  • Cost of Marketing Activities = 50,000

The calculation would be: 100,000 / 50,000 = 2.0

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

The Marketing Efficiency Ratio (MER) helps you measure the return on investment (ROI) from your marketing activities. A higher ratio indicates a more efficient marketing spend.

The formula for MER is: Revenue from Marketing Activities / Cost of Marketing Activities. This provides a ratio that reflects how much revenue is generated for every unit of cost spent on marketing.

A high Marketing Efficiency Ratio signifies that your marketing activities are yielding a high return relative to the cost. It helps businesses optimize their marketing budget and increase profitability.
