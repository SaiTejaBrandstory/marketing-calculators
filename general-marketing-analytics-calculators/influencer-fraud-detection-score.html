Influencer Fraud Detection Score

Formula:

The formula for calculating the Influencer Fraud Detection Score is:

Score = (Engagement Rate × 0.5) + (Audience Quality × 0.3) + (Content Authenticity × 0.2)

Example Calculation:

If:

  • Engagement Rate = 6%
  • Audience Quality = 80%
  • Content Authenticity = 75%

Score = (6 × 0.5) + (80 × 0.3) + (75 × 0.2) = 3 + 24 + 15 = 42

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

The Influencer Fraud Detection Score evaluates the authenticity and credibility of an influencer based on their engagement rate, audience quality, and content authenticity.

This score helps determine if an influencer is potentially engaged in fraudulent activities like buying fake followers or using manipulated engagement, providing brands with insights to make informed marketing decisions.

To lower the fraud detection score, influencers should focus on genuine audience engagement, improving content authenticity, and ensuring their followers are real and relevant to their niche.
