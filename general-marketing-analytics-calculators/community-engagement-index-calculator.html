Community Engagement Index Calculator

Formula:

The formula for calculating the community engagement index:

Engagement Index = (Likes × 0.4) + (Shares × 0.3) + (Comments × 0.2) + (Mentions × 0.1)

Example Calculation:

If:

  • Likes = 200
  • Shares = 150
  • Comments = 100
  • Mentions = 50

Engagement Index = (200 × 0.4) + (150 × 0.3) + (100 × 0.2) + (50 × 0.1) = 140

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

The Community Engagement Index is a metric that measures the level of interaction between a community and its content, based on likes, shares, comments, and mentions.

To improve your engagement index, focus on creating content that encourages likes, shares, comments, and mentions, and engage with your community through interactive posts.

A higher engagement index indicates a more engaged community. Scores above 100 are considered good, but this can vary depending on the platform and industry.
