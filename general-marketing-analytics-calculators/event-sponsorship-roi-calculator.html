Event Sponsorship ROI Calculator

Formula:

The formula for calculating Event Sponsorship ROI:

ROI (%) = [(Revenue from Sponsorship - Cost of Sponsorship) / Cost of Sponsorship] × 100

Example Calculation:

If:

  • Revenue from Sponsorship = ₹500,000
  • Cost of Sponsorship = ₹200,000

ROI (%) = [(500,000 - 200,000) / 200,000] × 100 = 150%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Event Sponsorship ROI measures the return on investment for a sponsorship by comparing revenue generated with the sponsorship cost.

To improve ROI, choose events aligned with your target audience, track engagement, negotiate better sponsorship deals, and follow up with attendees effectively.

A positive ROI (above 100%) means the sponsorship was profitable. Many companies aim for at least 200% ROI for high-value sponsorships.
