Sentiment Analysis ROI Predictor

Formula:

The formula for calculating Sentiment Analysis ROI is:

ROI (%) = ((Revenue Impact - Cost of Analysis) / Cost of Analysis) × 100

Example Calculation:

For example, if:

  • Revenue Impact = ₹1,00,000
  • Cost of Analysis = ₹20,000

The calculation would be: ((1,00,000 - 20,000) / 20,000) × 100 = 400%

Input for Sentiment Analysis ROI

Revenue Impact (₹)
Cost of Analysis (₹)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Sentiment Analysis ROI measures the return on investment for implementing sentiment analysis tools, comparing the financial impact against its cost.

It is calculated using the formula: ((Revenue Impact - Cost of Analysis) / Cost of Analysis) × 100.

It helps businesses evaluate whether their sentiment analysis investment is yielding financial benefits and assists in optimizing marketing and customer experience strategies.
