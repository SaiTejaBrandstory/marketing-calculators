Conversion Rate for Discounted Products

This tool helps calculate the conversion rate for discounted products based on factors like original price, discount price, and the number of products sold during the promotion.

Formula:

Conversion Rate = (Number of Products Sold / Total Visitors) * 100

Example Calculation:

Number of Products Sold: 150

Total Visitors: 5000

Conversion Rate = (150 / 5000) * 100 = 3%

Number of Products Sold
