Flash Sale Performance Predictor

This tool predicts the potential performance of your flash sale based on several factors like sales volume, customer engagement, and product scarcity.

Formula:

Flash Sale Performance = (Sales Volume + Customer Engagement + Product Scarcity) / 3

Example Calculation:

Sales Volume: 85

Customer Engagement: 70

Product Scarcity: 90

Flash Sale Performance = (85 + 70 + 90) / 3 = 81.67
